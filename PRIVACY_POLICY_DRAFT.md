# Privacy Policy — Agisano (Pty) Ltd

**Last updated:** 5 July 2026
**Applies to:** agisano.com and all services provided by Agisano (Pty) Ltd

Agisano (Pty) Ltd (registration number 2023/872204/07) ("Agisano", "we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting your personal information and respecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, share, and protect personal information in compliance with the **Protection of Personal Information Act, 4 of 2013 (POPIA)** of South Africa.

Because we serve schools, we handle personal information in **two distinct roles**, and different rules apply to each:

- As a **Responsible Party** — when we collect information directly from you through our website, our contact form, and our own business dealings (Sections 1–9).
- As an **Operator** (processor) — when we handle personal information *on behalf of a client school* while delivering managed IT services, building or hosting a school website, or supplying and configuring equipment (Section 10).

---

## 1. Who we are

Agisano (Pty) Ltd is a South African company registered with the CIPC (registration number 2023/872204/07), operating from Gauteng and providing ICT services to schools — including internet and WiFi installation, ICT equipment supply, managed IT services, and digital presence (websites and newsletters).

We are the **Responsible Party** under POPIA for personal information we collect through agisano.com and in the course of our own business.

**Information Officer:** Our designated Information Officer can be contacted at **hello@agisano.com**. The Information Officer is registered with the Information Regulator of South Africa and is responsible for ensuring our compliance with POPIA.

## 2. What personal information we collect

We collect only the information we need for the purposes set out in this policy.

**When you contact us or book an assessment**, through our website form, email, or phone/WhatsApp, we collect:

- Your name and job title (for example, Principal, HOD, or IT Coordinator)
- Your school's name and location
- Your email address and phone number
- Any details about your school's ICT needs that you choose to share in your message

**When you visit agisano.com**, we may collect:

- Standard technical data (IP address, browser type, device, pages visited) via server logs
- Website usage data via analytics cookies — **only if you consent** (see Section 7)

We do **not** collect the *special personal information* defined in Section 26 of POPIA (such as religion, health, or biometric data) through our website, and we do not knowingly collect personal information directly from learners or children through our website.

> **Note on learner and staff data:** When Agisano handles information about learners, parents, or school staff, we do so **only as an Operator on behalf of the school**, under that school's instruction and a written agreement. Section 10 covers this.

## 3. Why we collect it

We use the personal information you provide to us as a Responsible Party for the following purposes only:

- To respond to your enquiry or assessment booking request
- To schedule and conduct an on-site assessment
- To prepare and send you a proposal, quote, or invoice
- To deliver and support the services you engage us for
- To keep records required for tax, accounting, and legal purposes
- To improve our website — **only** where you have consented to analytics cookies

We process this information on the lawful bases permitted by POPIA: your consent, the performance of a contract with you, our legitimate business interests, and compliance with the law.

---

## 4. How we share your information

We do **not** sell, rent, or trade your personal information, and we do not share it with third parties for their own marketing.

We share information only with:

- **Service providers (Operators) who process information on our behalf** — for example, our email, form-handling, hosting, invoicing, and helpdesk providers. These providers are bound by written agreements requiring them to protect your information and use it only for the services they provide to us. Our current providers may include email/CRM and form-handling services, website and application hosting, and accounting software.
- **Professional advisors** — such as our accountant or legal advisor, where necessary.
- **Law enforcement or regulators** — where we are required to do so by law, or to protect our legal rights.

### Cross-border transfers

Some of our service providers may store or process information on servers outside South Africa. Where this happens, we take reasonable steps to ensure the transfer complies with Section 72 of POPIA — meaning the recipient is subject to a law, binding agreement, or rules that provide an adequate level of protection substantially similar to POPIA.

## 5. How long we keep your information

We keep your personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfil the purpose it was collected for, and to meet our legal, tax, and accounting obligations.

- Enquiries that do not become engagements: retained for up to **1 year**, then deleted.
- Client and prospect records: retained for the duration of our relationship and for up to **5 years** after our last engagement (to meet tax and record-keeping requirements), then securely deleted or anonymised.
- Information we hold as an Operator: retained and deleted according to the client school's instructions and our agreement with them (see Section 10).

## 6. Your rights under POPIA

As a data subject, you have the right to:

- Request access to the personal information we hold about you
- Request correction of information that is inaccurate, misleading, or out of date
- Request deletion or destruction of your information where we no longer have a lawful reason to keep it
- Object, on reasonable grounds, to our processing of your information
- Withdraw your consent at any time (where processing is based on consent)
- Not be subject to direct marketing without your consent
- Lodge a complaint with the Information Regulator of South Africa

To exercise any of these rights, email **hello@agisano.com**. We will respond within a reasonable period, and no later than **30 days**, unless the law allows us longer.

**If your request concerns learner, parent, or staff information held by us on behalf of a school, please direct it to your school**, which is the Responsible Party for that information. We will support the school in responding.

## 7. Cookies

Our website uses cookies to function and, with your consent, to understand how it is used.

- **Essential cookies** — required for the website to work. These cannot be declined.
- **Analytics cookies** — help us understand how visitors use the site so we can improve it. These are set **only with your consent**.

You can withdraw your consent at any time by clearing your browser cookies and declining on your next visit, or by changing your browser settings to block cookies.

## 8. How we protect your information

We take reasonable and appropriate technical and organisational measures to safeguard personal information against loss, unauthorised access, and misuse, as required by Section 19 of POPIA. These include:

- HTTPS/TLS encryption for all data transmitted through our website
- Access controls and strong authentication on the systems we use
- Limiting access to personal information to those who need it to do their work
- Choosing service providers who maintain appropriate security safeguards

No system is perfectly secure, but we work to protect your information and to review our safeguards regularly.

### Breach notification

If a security compromise affects your personal information, we will notify the Information Regulator and you as soon as reasonably possible after becoming aware of it, in line with Section 22 of POPIA — unless doing so would impede a criminal investigation.

## 10. When Agisano acts as an Operator for a school

This section applies when Agisano processes personal information **on behalf of a client school** — not for our own purposes. This happens when we:

- Provide **managed IT services** (administering a school's network, devices, user accounts, email, or backups)
- **Build or host a school website** or newsletter that contains information about staff, learners, or parents
- **Supply and configure equipment** that stores or processes personal information

In these situations:

- The **school is the Responsible Party** and remains accountable for the personal information. Agisano is the **Operator** and processes the information **only on the school's documented instructions**.
- We process this information **strictly for the purpose of delivering the agreed service** — never for our own marketing or any unrelated purpose.
- We treat all such information as **confidential**, apply the security measures described in Section 8, and restrict access to authorised personnel only.
- We may engage **sub-operators** (for example, a hosting provider) only where permitted by our agreement with the school, and we hold them to equivalent obligations.
- We **notify the school without undue delay** if we become aware of any compromise affecting the information we hold for them, so the school can meet its own obligations under Section 22 of POPIA.
- On termination of our engagement, we **return or securely delete** the information as the school directs, subject to any legal retention requirement.
- Requests from data subjects (learners, parents, staff) are the **school's responsibility** to fulfil; we assist the school in doing so.

The specific terms governing each engagement are set out in the written services agreement (and, where required, a data processing addendum) between Agisano and the school. Where that agreement and this policy differ in respect of Operator processing, **the agreement prevails**.
